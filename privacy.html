隐私政策 · Privacy Policy

Off the Record（下称"本游戏"）· 最近更新 / Last updated: 2026-07

我们处理哪些数据 · What we process

  • 聊天与剧情文本：你在游戏中输入的对话会发送给 Anthropic（Claude API）以生成角色回复。Your in-game messages are sent to Anthropic (Claude API) to generate character replies.
  • 图片生成指令：生成角色/场景图片时，文字描述会发送给 fal.ai。Image prompts are sent to fal.ai to generate artwork.
  • 设备标识：一个随机生成的匿名设备 ID，用于每日用量配额与云端存档，不关联你的真实身份。A random anonymous device ID for usage quotas and cloud saves; not linked to your identity.
  • 游戏存档：进度保存在你的设备（localStorage），并按设备 ID 备份到我们的服务器（Cloudflare KV）。Saves live on your device and are backed up to our server (Cloudflare KV) keyed by device ID.
  • 举报内容：你举报的 AI 内容片段会连同设备 ID 上报，仅用于内容安全核查。Reported AI content is uploaded with the device ID solely for safety review.

我们不做什么 · What we don't do

  • 不出售、不共享你的数据用于广告。We do not sell your data or share it for advertising.
  • 不收集真实姓名、通讯录、位置等身份信息。We do not collect your real name, contacts, or location.
  • 无第三方广告 SDK、无跟踪像素。No ad SDKs, no tracking pixels.

AI 生成内容 · AI-generated content

游戏中的角色对话与图片由 AI 生成，属虚构内容。你可以在聊天中长按任意角色消息进行举报，或通过下方邮箱反馈。Character dialogue and artwork are AI-generated fiction. Long-press any character message in chat to report it, or contact us below.

年龄限制 · Age

本游戏含成人向情感内容，仅适合 17 岁及以上玩家。This game contains mature romantic themes and is intended for players 17 and older.

数据保留与删除 · Retention & deletion

云端存档与配额记录会定期过期清理；如需立即删除你的云端数据，发邮件告知你的设备 ID（游戏内设置可查）即可。Cloud saves and quota records expire periodically; email us your device ID to delete your cloud data immediately.
